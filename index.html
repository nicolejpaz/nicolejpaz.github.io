Cofounder/Deployed

Fremote: Watch videos simultaneously!
Fremote is a web app to remote-control YouTube & Vimeo videos. A user can send a link to a friend(or friends) they are chatting with and they can watch the video/movie at the same time. Whoever has control of the "remote" can start/pause/fast-forward/rewind the video and it will control the video for every viewer.

Currently Planning

Fridgerator: My experiment in NoSQL. It's a simple app that allows a user to name the ingredients they have in their fridge (and cabinets), take out ingredients, and put more in as needed. There are plans to expand it so that it will allow adding recipes, and possibly meal plans. Reason for delay: I have to properly understand how to test MongoDB with RSpec, as well as become more familiar with the concepts behind NoSQL.

Look Up Genetics: Ever since 23andme was forced to take away some of their functionality, there is a gaping hole that needs to be filled. This will allow doctors to easily look up any medical problems that may arise based off of a 23andme report. Reason for this: doctors have a hard time finding this information themselves, and it's important for a patient to know. My cousin is helping me find more information on the subject, and I'm currently scouring assorted online information and libraries in order to learn more about genetic testing.
